Chingu Commentaries

Read about the commentaries of some of our chingu to their favorite Kdrama's. Don't be shy and share yours too!!

True Beauty is one of my favorite Korean dramas. The reasons are as follows: first, the story is extremely interesting. Lim Ju-gyeong (Moon Ga Young) was raised to be insecure and self-conscious about her appearance. She becomes famous after mastering the art of makeup and transforming herself into a 'pretty girl.' She refuses to show her bare face to anyone, despite her newfound popularity at school. However, Lee Suho (Cha Eun-woo) accepted her even though he is aware of Ju-gyeong's little secret and their love story begins there. The second point to mention is that the cast did an excellent job in their roles. The third point is the story's lesson. This kdrama taught me to appreciate yourself. Because there will always be at least one person who will love and accept you. If I'm going to rate it, I'm going to rate it, I will give 10/10. It become my source of happiness.

- Erika

Vincenzo was released a couple of months ago and ended yesterday, May 2, but the after effect of the series impacted me until now. It made me reflect that there is something good about being evil. It is a matter of choosing where is the lesser evil. Furthermore, it opened my eyes about the justice systems and how the media works in a certain nation. The power of manipulation is far enough to control the system, even in a blink of an eye and a snap in the fingers.

-Francis Aillah

Reply 1988- isa sa pinakapaborito kong kdrama at pinakamasarap ulit-uliting panoorin. Marahil itong palabas na ito ay kumpleto sa rekados na lahat ay binigyan ng pansin ang pagkakaibigan, pamilya, pagsubok at pangarap. Sa panonood ko nito marami akong natutunan na mga bagay tulad na lang sa pagmamahal sa magulang, kapatid, kaibigan at higit rito sa pamilya. Dahil kahit dumating tayo sa puntong ang mundo ay hindi na nakikisang-ayon sa gusto nating mangyari, hindi tayo mawawalan ng pag-asa sapagkat may kakapitan tayo at sila ang ating pamilya. Isa pa rito sa nagustuhan ko ay piliin natin maging masaya dahil tayo ay mabubuhay lang ng isang beses at hindi ito masama..

- Dolor

Vincenzo is a kdrama that has less romance but full of action, thriller, and dark humor. This drama is highly recommended for those people like me who love to watch action films. Vincenzo is one of the best korean drama that I've ever watched.

- Nazreen Joy

I can’t count the number of times I’ve paused a K-drama just to rewind to a crucial moment and watch it blow my mind again. As easily as these series convince you to root for the characters, they also deliver memorable life lessons and insights with lines that pack a punch. Whether it’s dealing with life’s hardships or taking a second chance at life, many series touch on the value of believing in yourself and standing up for what’s right. Critically acclaimed dramas like Itaewon Class, for instance, remind you not to give up on your dreams or think less of your ambitions, no matter what your detractors might say.

- Iday
