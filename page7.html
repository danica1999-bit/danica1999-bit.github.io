FIGHT FOR MY WAY

This drama will tell you that “there is no late time in pursuing your dream.” This drama shows how they grow up and do not achieve their dream and shows that there is a right time for everything, and do not give up on your dream. The friendship to all the characters wants you to say it is a ‘friendship goal,’ Dong Man, Aera, Seol Hee, and Joo Man, a 20 years long time friendship.

Though the love story between Dong Man and Aera is so much fun, and it excites me. I love how Dong Man supports Aera for her dream and always there for her when she has a problem.

THE LEGEND OF THE BLUE SEA

This kdrama is amazing. To be honest, I watched this many times, and still, it always makes me cry, laugh and kilig over and over again. So. this drama is about a mermaid Sim Chung goes to the land to look for Heo Jon Jae, the love of her life but the problem is she erased the memory of him because of their ill-fated destiny. The funny side of Sim Chung draws me into this drama. The actor and actress really portray their character very well.

WEIGHTLIFTING FAIRY KIM BOK JOO

This drama makes me fall in love with Bok Joo and Jung Hyun; it is really cute and feels real. I love how I see sparks with their character ( I literary shipped them ). The friendship that Kim Bok Joo had with Nan Hee and Seon Ok is super fantastic. I really love them! This drama tells about the college athletes students on how they achieved their dreams as an athlete and about the first love of weightlifter Kim Bok Joo.
